# Baseball Stats Tracker - Sales Copy

Objection handling, VSL opener, and sales-call close for the $39 one-time youth baseball stats app.

---

## 1. Objections + One-Sentence Reframes

### Price

1. **"$39 is a lot for an app."** → That's less than two months of GameChanger Premium, and you only pay it once.
2. **"I can find something free."** → Free apps make their money by selling you upgrades the minute you actually need them.
3. **"GameChanger is free, why switch?"** → GameChanger is free until you want spray charts, real stats, or no ads — then it's $5/month forever.
4. **"What if I don't use it enough?"** → If you use it for one season your kid will play in, you've already gotten your $39 back in stats you'd otherwise lose.

### Trust

5. **"Never heard of you."** → That's exactly why we don't have a subscription — we have to earn it on day one, not lock you in.
6. **"What if you go out of business?"** → Your stats export to CSV and your account isn't tied to a recurring charge, so nothing disappears on you.
7. **"Is my kid's data safe?"** → Your team's data lives in your private account, isn't sold, and isn't shared with scouts or third parties.
8. **"No reviews yet."** → You're early, which is also why this is still $39 instead of what it'll cost once we have 10,000 users.

### Switching cost

9. **"I already use GameChanger / iScore."** → You can run both for one game and decide — most parents switch after they see how much faster the scoring is.
10. **"My league requires GameChanger for the schedule."** → Keep GameChanger for the schedule, use this for the stats that actually matter to your kid.
11. **"I don't want to learn another app."** → If you can text, you can score a game in this — the average parent is live-scoring within one inning.

### Features

12. **"Will it work for travel ball / high school / Little League?"** → Same rules, same stats — it works for every level from 8U through adult rec.
13. **"Does it work offline at the field?"** → It scores offline and syncs the moment you're back on signal, so dead-zone fields aren't a problem.
14. **"Can grandparents follow live?"** → Yes — share a link and anyone, anywhere, can watch the box score update in real time.
15. **"Will it work on my phone?"** → It runs in any browser, so it works on iPhone, Android, iPad, and even your laptop in the bleachers.

### Time / behavior

16. **"Scoring pulls me out of watching the game."** → Most parents say the opposite — they finally stop missing at-bats because their head isn't down in a paper book doing math.
17. **"Paper is faster."** → Paper is faster until someone asks for the season OBP and you spend Sunday with a calculator.

### Person fit

18. **"I'm not the techy parent."** → If you've ever used DoorDash, you already know how to use this — there's nothing to install.
19. **"The coach handles stats."** → Then send the coach the link — most coaches are thrilled to stop being the bottleneck.
20. **"My kid is too young to care."** → They will the first time they see their own spray chart — that's usually the moment it clicks.

### Risk / authority / timing

21. **"What if I buy it and don't like it?"** → It's $39 once — less than two months of what GameChanger charges, so the worst case is you spent a tank of gas to find out.
22. **"I need to ask my spouse."** → Totally fair — most spouses say yes the second they hear "no subscription."
23. **"Season's almost over."** → Lock in this season's final stats now so your kid has a real baseline going into next year.
24. **"Let me think about it."** → Take the night — but score tomorrow's game in it first so you're deciding on real experience, not a guess.
